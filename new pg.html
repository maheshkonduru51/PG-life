About Tirupathi

Tirupathi - The Spiritual Capital

Tirupathi is a major pilgrimage city in the Indian state of Andhra Pradesh. It is home to the famous Sri Venkateswara Temple situated on the Tirumala Hills, one of the most visited religious sites in the world.

The city welcomes millions of devotees, students, and tourists every year. Finding suitable accommodation is crucial for a comfortable stay in this holy city. Whether you're visiting for darshan, education, or work, having a good PG accommodation enhances your experience.

Our platform connects newcomers to Tirupathi with verified, comfortable PG accommodations across different areas of the city, catering to various budgets and requirements.

Frequently Asked Questions

What documents do I need to book a PG?
Most PGs require a valid ID proof (Aadhar Card, Passport, Driving License), current address proof, passport size photographs, and sometimes a letter from your college or workplace. Some may also request advance payment and security deposit.
Are meals included in the PG rent?
It depends on the PG. Most of our listed PGs offer meal plans included in the rent. You can use our filters to find PGs with meal options that match your preferences. Some PGs offer vegetarian, non-vegetarian, or both options.
How far are these PGs from Tirumala Temple?
The distance varies based on the location of the PG. PGs in Tirumala Hills area are within 1-3 km of the temple. PGs in Tirupathi city are approximately 20-25 km from the temple, but have good transportation facilities to reach Tirumala.
Is WiFi available in all PGs?
Most of our PGs offer WiFi connectivity, but the speed and reliability may vary. You can check the amenities section of each PG listing to confirm if WiFi is provided. Some PGs may charge an additional fee for WiFi access.
Can I book a PG for short-term stay?
Yes, many PGs accommodate short-term stays, especially for pilgrims and tourists. The minimum stay duration may vary from PG to PG, and short-term rates might be higher than monthly rates. You can filter for short-term options in our search.
